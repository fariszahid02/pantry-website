About Us

We are second-year Computer Science students from Newcastle University.

Our app, PantryPal, aims to help users manage their pantry efficiently, reduce food waste, and discover new recipes.

View and Manage Food Items

With PantryPal, you can easily view the food you have in stock, keep track of expiry dates, and filter items based on calorie content and expiration dates. This helps in planning meals and reducing food waste effectively.

Recipes

Discover new recipes or create your own. PantryPal allows you to browse recipes, save your favorites, and even generate shopping lists based on the ingredients needed for your chosen recipes.

Shopping Lists

Create and manage shopping lists effortlessly. Once you complete a shopping list, the items are automatically added to your pantry inventory, making it easy to keep track of what you have on hand.

Scan Barcodes

Discover the easy way to add to your pantry. PantryPal allows you to scan barcodes to speed up adding new foods.

Check your calories

PantryPal provides filters for your pantry and recipes to help you stay on top of your health goals.

Inventory Management

Keep track of your ingredients, expiry dates, and quantities.

Shopping Lists

Create and manage shopping lists directly from your pantry items.

Analytics

Gain insights into usage patterns and optimize your pantry organization.
